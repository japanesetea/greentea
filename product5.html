Itoen Oi Ocha Green Tea

Itoen Oi Ocha Green Tea Bags are an excellent value for money and are perfect for green tea lovers who want to enjoy high-quality Japanese green tea's natural flavor and aroma. The tea bags are individually wrapped to ensure freshness and convenience, making it easy to enjoy green tea anytime, anywhere.


Health benefits:

  • Helps to boost immune system
  • Improve digestion
  • Reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as cancer and heart disease

Contents and Price:
  • 2.0 g x 20 bags: 700 TAKA

Making:

Being an instant tea bag, Itoen Oi Ocha Green Tea can be easily consumed anytime, anywhere.

For cold water: Pour 150 ml of cold water into a cup and keep one bag of Itoen Oi Ocha Green Tea in it for 3 to 4 minutes. During this time, stir the tea bag inside the cup 5 to 6 times.


For hot water: Pour 150 ml of boiling water into a cup and place one bag of Itoen Oi Ocha Green Tea for 30 seconds. During this time, stir the tea bag inside the cup 5 to 6 times. Your green tea is ready to drink.
